The main goal was to find:

---

Key Insights

From this analysis, I found:

- Sales department has highest attrition
- Overtime is the biggest reason employees leave
- Low salary increases attrition
- Delayed promotions increase attrition
- New employees are more likely to quit early

---

Business Recommendations

Based on the findings:

- Reduce overtime workload
- Improve salary structure
- Provide better work-life balance
- Improve promotion opportunities
- Focus retention programs for high-risk departments
